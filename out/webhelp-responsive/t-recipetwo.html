Kale and Strawberry Smoothie

This smoothie is an easy breakfast that is full of all your essential nutrients for the day. The healthy greens are hardly noticeable with the addition of naturally sweet fruits.

For this recipe you will need a blender. The following ingredients will also be needed?
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup washed and dried kale leaves
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1/d cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 scoop vegan protein powder
  • Prep time: 2 minutes
  • Assembly time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients to the blender.
    CAUTION:
    Be sure that the blender is unplugged and in an "off" position while adding ingredients.
  2. Blend on a the highest speed for 30 seconds then switch blender off
  3. Remove lid and pour the smoothie into a glass
    CAUTION:
  4. Pour smoothie into glass
