Bagles

A warm and toasty bagel is a great platform to get creative and build a hearty breakfast. A bagel is easy to take on your commute during busy days, or to enjoy inside the apartment on slow mornings. .

This recipe requires a skillet, a toaster and a knife. The following ingredients will also be needed:
  • 3 slices smoked salmon, chilled
  • 3-4 tomoato slices cut into halves
  • 1 tablespoon of hummus
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Salt and papper to taste
  • 1 whole wheat bagel, sliced and toasted
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Assembly time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1

Directions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced bell pepper with salt and pepper to taste. Stir the bell peppers occaisionally and allow to cook for 5-10 minutes to cook
    1. Drain the bell peppers of any excess oil after cooking is complete
    2. Allow bell peppers to cool before assembling with the bagel
  3. Toast the sliced bagel in the toaster for two minutes or until golden brown
  4. To assemble, spread each side of the bagel with cream cheese and top with the cooked bell peppers and smoked salmon
  5. To assemlbe, spread each side of the bagel with hummus and top with the cooked bell peppers and sliced tomatos
  6. Squeeze lemon over the bagel
  7. Serve immediately
